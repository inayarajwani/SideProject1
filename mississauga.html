Square One Shopping Centre

Square One is a shopping mall and is the largest shopping centre in Ontario and the second largest shopping centre in Canada. The mall features a variety of stores from Holt Renfrew and Tiffany&Co. to Garage and Forever 21. The recent expansion in January 2013 brought about the construction of multiple wings and, in turn, many new stores, such as Holt Renfrew. To this day, the mall is expanding and is increasing popularity. For more information, click here.

Scooter's Roller Palace

The popular roller skating rink is a 24,500 sq. ft. facility that first opened its doors in 1975. Scooter's Roller Palace has interested and captured the public's attention to the retro concept of roller skating through the massive building's modern, relaxing and unique aestheticn.
